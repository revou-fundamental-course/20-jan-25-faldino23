Important Information

Asian populations typically have different body compositions compared to Western populations. Research has shown that Asian people generally have a higher percentage of body fat than Western people of the same age, sex, and BMI. This is why the BMI cutoff points for overweight and obesity are lower for Asian populations.

The World Health Organization (WHO) and International Obesity Task Force (IOTF) recognize that the risk of type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease is higher at lower BMI levels in Asian populations compared to European populations.
